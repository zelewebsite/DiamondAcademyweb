WHY CHOOS US

Introduction

Diamond Academy is a prestigious educational institution located in the heart of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Renowned for its commitment to academic excellence, holistic development, and community engagement, Diamond Academy offers a vibrant and enriching environment for students from diverse backgrounds.

Vision:

Diamond Academy aspires to be a leading institution in Ethiopia, fostering academic excellence, innovative thinking, and ethical leadership among its students. The academy aims to prepare its students to be globally competent and socially responsible citizens.

Mission:

The mission of Diamond Academy is to provide high-quality education that nurtures intellectual growth, personal development, and a lifelong love of learning. The academy is dedicated to creating a supportive and inclusive community where every student is encouraged to reach their full potential.

Extracurricular Activities

Sports and Athletics

he academy offers a wide range of sports, including basketball, soccer, swimming, tennis, and track and field.Facilities include state-of-the-art gymnasiums, sports fields, and swimming pools.Regular inter-school competitions and tournaments keep the athletic spirit high.

Arts and Culture

There are numerous opportunities for students to engage in the arts, such as drama, music, dance, and visual arts.Students can participate in school productions, concerts, and art exhibitions.Workshops and guest lectures from industry professionals enhance the learning experience.

Clubs and Societies

The academy hosts various clubs and societies catering to diverse interests like debate, robotics, environmental activism, and more.These clubs provide a platform for students to pursue their passions, develop leadership skills, and collaborate with peers..

Academic Excellence Curriculum: Diamond Academy offers a rigorous and comprehensive curriculum that spans from early childhood education to secondary school. The curriculum is designed to challenge students intellectually while providing them with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in higher education and beyond. Key areas of focus include: Sciences and Mathematics: Emphasis on critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Humanities and Social Sciences: Encouragement of analytical and creative thinking. Languages: Strong programs in English, Amharic, and additional foreign languages. Technology and Innovation: Integration of modern technology and innovative teaching methods. Faculty: The academy prides itself on its team of highly qualified and dedicated educators who bring a wealth of experience and expertise to the classroom. Faculty members are committed to fostering a supportive learning environment and mentoring students to achieve academic success. Innovation and Technology Technology Integration: Use of the latest technology in teaching and learning. Classrooms equipped with interactive whiteboards and digital learning tools. Innovation Labs: Dedicated spaces for students to explore STEM fields and work on hands-on projects. Access to cutting-edge technology, including 3D printers and robotics kits. Events and Traditions Annual Events: Science fairs, cultural festivals, sports days, and talent shows. Events that bring the community together and provide platforms for students to showcase their talents.
